# A Thunder of Dragons — GN #3 ("The Tournament") — Page-by-Page Beat Sheet

**Adapts:** Volume One, **Book Three**
**Role:** The great convergence — the whole cast collides at the Madison Square Garden tournament while
the mythology's true origin (1928) surfaces. Secrets break open, the fight bracket cross-cuts with the
Flick/T-Byrd pool game, and the night curdles into violence. Ends on the volume's hardest cliffhanger:
**Genee slammed by a car.**
**Status:** Working draft for Louis's review (canon authority). Beats and page counts are a proposal.

---

## Format assumptions

- **Target length:** ~**64 story pages** (center of a 58–70 range).
- **Splittable:** if serialized, breaks cleanly into **two ~32-page issues** — recommended break noted
  at the "…and then there were eight" cliffhanger.
- **Signature device:** the tournament **intercuts the fight bracket with the Flick vs. T-Byrd pool
  game** — Louis's script explicitly calls for "a creative tour de force… weaving the pool game with
  the fights." Preserve that cross-cut on the page.
- **Two caption voices continue:** adult-Jay's narration + Remi's distinct captions.
- **How to read:** `p#` (ranges for sequences), with **[money page]**, *(art note)*, *(pitch logic)*.

---

## What GN #3 must accomplish

1. **Deliver the convergence** — every lead the reader has followed for two books finally shares one
   room; the payoff of all that cross-cutting.
2. **Show the mythology's origin** — 1928 makes the red-energy conflict concrete, tying the buried
   craft (GN #1) and the apocalypse (GN #2) to a single event.
3. **Break the big secrets** — Remi's full bloodline; Carly's a cop — earned, not dumped.
4. **Bleed myth into reality** — Kayam's gold miracle: the supernatural steps on-stage in public.
5. **End on the gut-punch** — Genee's accident, the hardest cliffhanger in the volume.

## COLD OPEN — 1928, CHINA (pp 1–6)

*(pitch logic: the mythology's origin, finally shown — pays off the buried craft from GN #1 and the
apocalypse from GN #2.)*

- **pp1–2** — A forested mountain ridge; three young monks — **Lu, Yu, Jin** — see something blaze
  overhead, then a massive explosion.
- **pp3–4** — A huge crater; a strange metallic craft. Fearless **Jin** climbs down — *"Is not our
  purpose as Shaolin Monks to understand that which we do not?"* — and clears the **recurring symbol**
  (the one from GN #1's subway).
- **pp5–6** — [money page] Jin **touches** it; the craft lights; **Jin and the craft glow red**; a bolt
  of red lightning cracks to the sky and spiderwebs across the land. *(art note: match exactly to GN
  #1's '181' craft and GN #2's apocalypse — the same force, fifty years apart.)*

## ACT I — ASSEMBLING THE CAST (pp 7–12)

- **pp7–8** — Jay & Chuy head to MSG; the *"a **thunder** of dragons"* collective-noun bit ("a KII-YAH
  of Kung-FOOL-ery"). Remi buses in with Victor & Javi; Nick wakes Carly ("Blacky Chan in the house");
  Sean & Lian.

  shop later burns.)*

## ACT II — THE SPECTACLE & THE BRACKET (pp 13–44)

**The Aaron Banks show (pp 13–24)**
- **pp13–15** — Banks emcees; Chuy's *"ORIENTAL?"* ("woke before woke"); demonstrations — Shuri-te
  board-breaking; the **Samurai jugglers Kuwabatake & Ushitora** (*Louis's note: remember them in
  Volume Four*); blindfolded Sumatran archers.
- **pp16–18** — **Swift Deer's Chulukua-Ryu** display; Victor's explanation of the free-flow style
  **intercut with Lozen** taking a cavalry battalion. *(pitch logic: Remi's power gets its on-page
  thesis before she fights.)*
- **pp19–20** — **Bongo the Mongol (Kublai Kayam)** headbutts granite — knocks himself cold. Nick &
  Carly's running bet begins.
- **pp21–24** — The **Shaolin debut**: Master Lu and the Temple; judges **Van Clief & Chiba**. The pool
  game opens — **Flick vs. T-Byrd** — *"Miss one shot… inevitable."*

**The bracket, 256 → 8 (pp 25–44)** *(cross-cut with the pool game throughout)*
- **pp25–32** — The Open Round: Chuy's Ali-shuffle KO of M'Tingwa; Sean, Carly, Remi, **Hoon** (a
  beast), Nick, Junior, and Jay ("HOO-KIKT yo ass") all advance; Petra Von Hookikt; Kayam's second
  self-KO. Flick runs the table as T-Byrd waits.
- **pp33–36** — [money page] **Swift Deer reveals Remi's full lineage** — Lozen → a daughter →
  **Remedia (Remi's mother)** → Remi; the old photo, *"you have her eyes."* *(the emotional peak of the
  book.)*
- **pp37–40** — Deeper rounds; **Carly is revealed to be an undercover cop.** **Genee sneaks out** into
  her bad night. Cochran and Laura at Spofford.
- **pp41–44** — Quarters & semis: **Jay vs. Remi** (a mutual-respect classic; *"Mama didn't say
  anything about a FOOT"*); **Hoon vs. Sean** (Hoon wins); Master Lu noting everyone.

## ACT III — THE FINAL, THE MIRACLE & THE TURN (pp 45–64)

**The final (pp 45–52)**
- **pp45–48** — [money page] **The Final: Hoon vs. Jay.** *"1500 years of tradition against a kid from
  Harlem."* Jay is KO'd, rallies (the Mrs Jones / James Brown "Sex Machine" flashback), fights to a
  tie — but **Hoon wins.** *"That fantasy was over…"*
- **pp49–50** — Banks presents the **eight finalists** (Jay, Hoon, Sean, Remi, Chuy, Nick, Carly,
  Junior); Master Lu bows to the New Yorkers; Jay to Junior: *"Keep it up. You got a future."*
- **pp51–52** — [money page] **Kayam returns, chanting — glows GOLD — and shatters the granite.** The
  mythology bleeds into the "real" world in front of everyone. *(art note: the gold echoes Xia's golden
  child and the apocalypse's charged figures.)* *(If serializing, recommended two-issue break here — on
  "and then there were eight" + the gold miracle.)*

**The night turns (pp 53–64)**
- **pp53–54** — The pool game resolves: **T-Byrd sinks the 8 and takes Flick's money** — *"the rest is
  inevitable."* Flick: *"This shit is mine."*
- **pp55–56** — Outside: Sean & Lian are surrounded by Italians (a bat swings); Sean, Jay & Chuy clock
  Remi as the one who surprised them all.
- **pp57–58** — Nick & Carly are pulled over; the trunk; the suitcase — *"Lawyer."* Arman Arkadian's
  body floats in the Hudson.
- **pp59–60** — Nick's interrogation: **Agent Bearden**, the suitcase of planted guns, *"the 20 years
  you and your chica looking at."* Buster meets T-Byrd in the Impala — *"Give you that name…"*
- **pp61–62** — Master Lu resolves to do the thing *"never done before"* — recruit them. Balo's warning
  of imbalance.
- **pp63–64** — [money page] Jay & **Mrs Jones**' Zodiac marathon ("Tongue Fu") intercut with Spofford
  (Remi finds Laura's bed empty) — and the button: **GENEE IS SLAMMED BY A CAR.** *(the hardest
  cliffhanger in the volume — straight into GN #4.)*
