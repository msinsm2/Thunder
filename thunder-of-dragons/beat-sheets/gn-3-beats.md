# A Thunder of Dragons — GN #3 ("The Tournament & The Choosing") — Page-by-Page Beat Sheet

**Adapts:** Volume One, **Books Three + Four**
**Role:** The finale — the great convergence (the Madison Square Garden tournament), the mythology's
origin (1928), the violent crossing-of-lines that forges each lead, and the recruitment that launches
the whole cast into the Dragonverse. Ends on the jet to China.
**Status:** Working draft for Louis's review (canon authority). Beats and page counts are a proposal.

---

## ⚠️ Read this first — the length/break decision

This book fuses **two** of Louis's internal Books, so it's roughly **double** the length of GN #1 and
#2 — a **~106-page** finale. That's the known trade-off of the 3-graphic-novel plan (Recommendation A).
Before you take the breaks to Louis, there are **three viable cuts** — this is the key thing to examine:

1. **Keep it as one big finale (Recommendation A).** GN #3 = Books Three + Four, ~106pp. A long,
   climactic capstone. Simplest; honors the 3-book plan.
2. **Split at the Book Three | Book Four seam → a 4-graphic-novel plan.** The cleanest natural break in
   the whole volume: Book Three ends on **Genee slammed by a car** (p64 below). Make that GN #3, and
   Book Four becomes GN #4. Four evenly-sized books.
3. **Split mid-tournament (Recommendation B).** End the *previous* book on the **"and then there were
   eight"** finalists cliffhanger, moving the tournament's first half into GN #2. Balances all three but
   splits the tournament across two books.

The page-by-page below is written as the **single fused finale (option 1)**, with the **two seams
marked in bold** so any of the three cuts is a one-line decision.

---

## Format assumptions

- **Target length:** ~**106 story pages** as one book (or ~2× ~53pp books if split — see above).
- **Structure:** a **1928 cold open** (mythology origin) → **Book Three: the tournament** (the
  convergence) → **Book Four: the choosing** (the climax & launch).
- **Signature device:** the tournament intercuts the **fight bracket** with the **Flick vs. T-Byrd pool
  game** — Louis's script calls for "a creative tour de force… weaving the pool game with the fights."
  Preserve that cross-cut on the page.
- **How to read:** `p#` (ranges for sequences), with **[money page]**, *(art note)*, *(pitch logic)*,
  and **‹SEAM›** markers at the two split points.

## COLD OPEN — 1928, CHINA (pp 1–6)

*(pitch logic: the mythology's origin, finally shown — pays off the buried craft from GN #1 and the
apocalypse from GN #2.)*

- **pp1–2** — A forested mountain ridge; three young monks — **Lu, Yu, Jin** — see something blaze
  overhead and a massive explosion.
- **pp3–4** — A huge crater; a strange metallic craft. Fearless **Jin** climbs down — *"Is not our
  purpose as Shaolin Monks to understand that which we do not?"* — and reveals the **recurring symbol**
  (the one from GN #1's subway).
- **pp5–6** — [money page] Jin **touches** it; the craft lights; **Jin and the craft glow red**; a bolt
  of red lightning cracks to the sky and spiderwebs across the land. *(art note: match exactly to GN
  #1's '181' craft and GN #2's apocalypse — the same event, 50 years apart.)*

## ACT I — BOOK THREE: THE TOURNAMENT (pp 7–64)

**Assembling the cast (pp 7–12)**
- **pp7–8** — Jay & Chuy head to MSG; the "**thunder** = a collective of dragons" conversation ("a
  KII-YAH of Kung-FOOL-ery"). Remi, Victor & Javi bus in; Nick wakes Carly; Sean & Lian.
- **pp9–10** — **Daddy Green's Pizza** — Junior wanted off work; little brother **Ritchie** the rhymer;
  the 15-pies-to-Coney-Island order that springs Junior. *(seeds Junior's guilt when the shop burns.)*
- **pp11–12** — Inside the Garden: the wild crowd pouring in; the leads finding seats.

**The Aaron Banks spectacle (pp 13–24)**
- **pp13–15** — Banks emcees; Chuy's *"ORIENTAL?"* ("woke before woke"); demonstrations — Shuri-te
  board-breaking; the **Samurai jugglers Kuwabatake & Ushitora** (*Louis's note: remember them in
  Volume Four*); the blindfolded Sumatran archers.
- **pp16–18** — **Chulukua-Ryu / Swift Deer's** students perform; Victor's explanation of the free-flow
  style **intercut with Lozen** taking a cavalry battalion. *(pitch logic: Remi's power gets its
  on-page thesis.)*
- **pp19–20** — **Bongo the Mongol (Kublai Kayam)** headbutts granite — knocks himself cold. Nick &
  Carly's running bet begins.
- **pp21–24** — The **Shaolin debut**: Master Lu and the Temple; celebrity judges **Van Clief & Chiba**;
  the pool game opens — **Flick vs. T-Byrd** — *"Miss one shot… inevitable."*

**The bracket — 256 → 8 (pp 25–44)** *(cross-cut with the pool game throughout)*
- **pp25–32** — The Open Round: Chuy's Ali-shuffle KO of M'Tingwa; Sean, Carly, Remi, Hoon (a beast),
  Nick, Junior, Jay ("HOO-KIKT yo ass") all advance; Petra Von Hookikt; Kayam's second self-KO. Flick
  runs the table as T-Byrd waits.
- **pp33–36** — [money page] **Swift Deer reveals Remi's full lineage** — Lozen → a daughter → **Remedia
  (Remi's mother)** → Remi; the old photo, *"you have her eyes."* *(the emotional peak of Book Three.)*
- **pp37–40** — Deeper rounds; **Carly is revealed to be an undercover cop** (the seed of GN #3's Nick
  betrayal). **Genee sneaks out** into her bad night. Cochran and Laura at Spofford.
- **pp41–44** — Quarters & semis: **Jay vs. Remi** (mutual-respect classic; *"Mama didn't say anything
  about a FOOT"*); **Hoon vs. Sean** (Hoon wins); Master Lu noting everyone.

**The final & the miracle (pp 45–54)**
- **pp45–48** — [money page] **The Final: Hoon vs. Jay.** *"1500 years of tradition against a kid from
  Harlem."* Jay is KO'd, rallies (the Mrs Jones / James Brown "Sex Machine" flashback), fights to a
  tie — but **Hoon wins.** *"That fantasy was over…"*
- **pp49–50** — Banks presents the **eight finalists** (Jay, Hoon, Sean, Remi, Chuy, Nick, Carly,
  Junior); Master Lu bows to the New Yorkers; Jay to Junior: *"Keep it up. You got a future."*
- **pp51–52** — [money page] **Kayam returns, chanting — glows GOLD — and shatters the granite.** The
  mythology bleeds into the "real" world in front of everyone. *(art note: the gold echoes Xia's golden
  child + the apocalypse's charged figures.)* **‹SEAM — Recommendation B split point: end the prior
  book here, on "and then there were eight" + the gold miracle.›**
- **pp53–54** — Pool game resolves: **T-Byrd sinks the 8, takes Flick's money** — *"the rest is
  inevitable."* Flick: *"This shit is mine."*

**Post-tournament — the night turns (pp 55–64)**
- **pp55–56** — Outside: Sean & Lian surrounded by Italians (a bat swings); Sean, Jay & Chuy clock Remi
  as the one who surprised them all.
- **pp57–58** — Nick & Carly pulled over; the trunk; the suitcase — *"Lawyer."* Arman Arkadian's body
  floats in the Hudson.
- **pp59–60** — Nick's interrogation: **Agent Bearden**, the suitcase of planted guns, *"the 20 years
  you and your chica looking at."* Buster meets T-Byrd in the Impala — *"Give you that name…"*
- **pp61–62** — Master Lu resolves to do the thing *"never done before"* — recruit them. Balo's warning.
- **pp63–64** — Jay & **Mrs Jones**' Zodiac marathon ("Tongue Fu") intercut with Spofford (Remi finds
  Laura's bed empty) and — **‹SEAM — BOOK THREE ends: GENEE IS SLAMMED BY A CAR. (Option-2 split point:
  make this the end of GN #3; Book Four becomes GN #4.)›**

## ACT II — BOOK FOUR: THE CHOOSING (pp 65–106)

**The rage (pp 65–70)**
- **pp65–68** — [money page] Jay's **blood-red revenge fantasy** — killing Flick in the pool hall,
  taking Flick's bullet — intercut with him furiously packing (*"Don't think — just PACK. I'd given
  Mama my word."*).
- **pp69–70** — The reveal: it's a **dream** — Mrs Jones' closet, **Mr Jones** home. Comic release after
  the nightmare.

**The recruitment begins / the church (pp 71–78)**
- **pp71–72** — Spofford lockdown (Cochran punishes all). Hospital: Genee stable. Nick's deal — offer
  **Tinsley**; **Carly-the-cop confirmed** behind the glass.
- **pp73–75** — The church: *"DARK TIMES up here in Harlem… WHAT CAN I DO TO STAND AGAINST THE DARK?"*
  On **Junior**: the question takes root.
- **pp76–78** — The **scrolls** arrive — Hoon recruits **Chuy**, then **Sean**, then **Junior**; **Shih
  seeks Remi.** Jade to Chuy: *"get Jay on that plane… do not breathe a word of it to HIM."*

**Sean & Lian (pp 79–82)**
- **pp79–80** — Sean storms **Mr Zhao** (the massage parlor) for Lian — *"There is DARKNESS inside you,
  Feng!"*
- **pp81–82** — [money page] **Port Authority**: Lian leaves by bus — *"there is another you in there…
  full of a rage that terrifies me."* Sean: *"this was YOUR choice."*

**Jay's choice (pp 83–88)**
- **pp83–85** — Hospital: the truth — Genee **assaulted and drugged (Flick).** Jay overhears. Jade's
  plea: *"I am NOT gonna lose YOU like I did your Daddy… SWEAR to me, son."*
- **pp86–88** — Jay bolts for Flick; **Chuy tackles him** — *"This is not who we ARE, Jay!"*

**Remi's reckoning (pp 89–91)**
- **pp89–91** — [money page] Remi takes **Lozen's bow**, and **kills Cochran with an arrow between the
  eyes**; frees Laura — *"you keep singing, Laura Charles."* *(the payoff of the boxcutter, the guitar
  girl, and the Lozen lineage — all at once.)*

**The crossings converge (pp 92–100)**
- **p92** — Sean's **one-man war** vs. the Italians in the Mulberry St. crosswalk — bodies strewn; he
  walks away in a rage.
- **pp93–97** — **Café Figaro shootout**: Tinsley kills **Bearden**; **Nick kills Tinsley**; **Carly:
  "your father KILLED mine."** Nick walks out; Carly can't pull the trigger.
- **pp98–100** — [money page] The reveal that **Buster** gave T-Byrd the name — **Flick is arrested.**
  Jay & Chuy round the corner to see it; Jay's rage defused by another man's quiet stand: *"Darkness
  wins when a nigga like me know better don't do what needs to be done."*

**The choosing (pp 101–106)**
- **pp101–102** — **Junior → Sifu Yu**; he gives his real name: *"I am — Leroy."*
- **pp103–105** — [money page] The chosen — **Jay, Chuy, Remi, Sean, Nick** — converge at JFK like
  *"Reservoir Dragons."* Master Lu: *"in one way or another each of us HAD been chosen."*
- **p106** — [money page] The **red jet** rumbles down the runway. *"We were going to the Shaolin
  Temple… And we were about to find the fuck out."* **END OF VOLUME ONE.** *(hook into Volume Two.)*

## Split options summary (for the break review)

| Cut | GN #3 becomes | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **A — one fused finale** | pp 1–106 (Books III+IV) | Simplest; long capstone. The plan of record. |
| **B — mid-tournament** | prior book ends at p52 (the "eight" + gold miracle); GN #3 = finals→end | Balances 3 books; splits the tournament. |
| **2 → four books** | GN #3 = Book III (ends p64, Genee hit); **GN #4 = Book IV** | Cleanest natural seam; four even books. Best if page budget can't carry a 106pp finale. |

*(My read: if Louis is open to it, **option 2 (four books) is the most production-friendly** — four
~50-page books, each with a clean arc, and the "Genee hit by a car" cliffhanger is the strongest in the
volume. But it changes the plan from 3 books to 4, so it's his call.)*
