# A Thunder of Dragons — GN #2 ("Coming of Age") — Page-by-Page Beat Sheet

**Adapts:** Volume One, **Book Two**
**Role:** The escalation book — raises the stakes from street-level to cosmic (the future apocalypse
cold-open), deepens the ensemble, plants the crime and mythology webs, and delivers the birth of the
icon (Chuy's first sketch of the Shogun of Harlem). Ends with the Shaolin recruiters loose in the city
and the storm closing in.
**Status:** Working draft for Louis's review (canon authority). Beats and page counts are a proposal.

---

## Format assumptions

- **Target length:** ~**78 story pages** (Book Two is the densest of the four — sprawling ensemble +
  a big cosmic set piece — so treat 78 as the center of a 72–88 range).
- **Splittable:** if serialized, breaks cleanly into **two ~39-page issues** — recommended break noted
  at the midpoint.
- **Structure:** a cosmic **cold open** (the 1991 apocalypse) sets the stakes; a **"making of the
  Dragonverse" spine** (Jay & Chuy's comics, aging 14→18) is the connective heartbeat; the **1978
  ensemble threads** cross-cut toward the recruiters' arrival.
- **Two caption voices continue:** adult-Jay's retrospective narration + Remi's distinct captions.
- **How to read:** `p#` = one page (ranges for multi-page sequences), with the core beat and, where
  useful, **[money page]**, *(art note)*, or *(pitch logic)*.

---

## What GN #2 must accomplish

1. **Blow the stakes wide open** — the apocalypse cold-open tells a buyer this street saga is the seed
   of a world-ending, world-saving mythology.
2. **Show the ensemble growing up** — the kids become who they'll be; the reader's investment compounds.
3. **Birth the icon** — Chuy's first drawing of the Shogun of Harlem is the book's emotional/commercial
   center.
4. **Weave the two webs** — crime (Egan/Flick) and mythology (Xia/the storm) close in without an
   info-dump.
5. **End poised** — the Shaolin recruiters are in New York; the tournament is inevitable.

## COLD OPEN — THE THUNDER (pp 1–9) — 1991

*(pitch logic: opening GN #2 in the future is the escalation — after #1's street origins, the world
just went cosmic. This is the "here's the whole franchise's endgame" sequence.)*

- **p1** — [money page] Himalayas, night: an eagle over the **Jin Temple**; a bolt of **red lightning
  erupts UP** from within, spiderwebbing across the clouds; warrior monks gallop out. VOICE: *"It has
  begun…"*
- **p2** — NYC, Central Park, a normal sunny day — a dog barks at the sky; a menacing dark cloud rolls
  over the rooftops. A father scoops up his child and runs.
- **p3** — Times Square (marquees: *Terminator 2*, *New Jack City*); the sun is blacked out; confusion
  → panic.
- **p4** — The day becomes glowing-red night; **blood-red rain**; subway steps flood; TV anchors — *"the
  DOW is down 1500…"*
- **p5** — The red cloud over D.C. & the Pentagon (DEFCON; Colin Powell), then **Beijing** — warrior
  monks gallop through Tiananmen; soldiers fire; **bullets have no effect**.
- **p6** — Moscow, Paris, and the U.N. — Boutros-Ghali calls it **extraterrestrial** and pleads for the
  world to unite.
- **p7** — [money page] Five silhouetted figures raise their arms; red lightning "charges" them; **five
  glowing red fists** plunge into the earth; a chain reaction ripples across the globe. *(art note:
  rhyme these five with Chuy's premonition sketch on p71.)*
- **p8** — Hollywood, poolside: **Tupac**, unbothered — *"listen to the sky for a thunder of dragons…
  a WUXIA nigga out there gon' step up."* *(rights: real-person depiction — flagged in the breakdown.)*
- **p9** — Red lightning incinerates the reporter; the feed dies. Caption bridges home: *this is the
  world our baby dragons are being forged to save — and it starts thirteen years earlier…*

## ACT I — THE MAKING OF THE DRAGONVERSE (pp 10–20)

*(the connective heartbeat: Jay & Chuy age 14 → 18; the comics evolve; the friendship deepens.)*

- **pp10–11** — Takegawa's dojo, **14, orange belts** ("AGAIN!"). Jay's narration on Chuy: *"they
  clearly didn't see the guy I did."* The comic-store encyclopedia; the anger — *"all these heroes were
  WHITE… I'mma just make my OWN damn comics."*
- **p12** — **Sofrito & Swoosh** — the first crude sketches (radioactive-jalapeño fire breath; Swoosh
  the last sound a mofo hears). *(art note: the in-world comics get their own art style — a book inside
  the book.)*
- **p13** — **16, purple belts.** **Yinja & Yangsta'** — Ronin brothers vs. shape-shifting Yakuza
  Chupacabras and the glowing-red **BATSHITO**.
- **p14** — Jefferson table: Genee (13) — *"How come you got no girls up in here?"* → Chuy creates
  **DARQUARYA**, the dark goddess. Halloween in the costumes.
- **pp15–16** — **18, black belts.** **Enter the DragonVerse** — *"Tiburón Kataña and the Shogun of
  Alpha Centauri"*: an inter-dimensional war of light vs. darkness; Kataña always has the Shogun's
  back. Darquarya's backstory (Queen Goddess of darkness, out of a Black Hole, to consume the light).
  *(pitch logic: Chuy is drawing their real future in comic-book code — the reader clocks it before
  they do.)*
- **pp17–18** — Roof, weed, tagging trains, tournament reps — *"I can't help wondering if Chuy had some
  actual secret super power to see the future."*
- **pp19–20** — [money page] The storm imagery returns (wolves, the misted monastery, red lightning,
  the Times Square blackout) — *"comic-book fantasy or premonition of the very real storm gathering just
  beyond the horizon?"* Bridges the cold open to the mythology.

## ACT II — 1978: THE WALLS CLOSE IN (pp 21–58)

**Sean — the fuse**
- **p21** — HS cafeteria: Sean (18) & **Lian**; the "Goldilocks of dicks" rant. Italians: *"We know
  where you live, Feng."* *(seeds Sean's war in GN #3.)*

**Remi — the caged warrior (Book Two's emotional heavyweight)**
- **pp22–23** — Spofford yard: Remi counts six years (72 months, 312 weeks…); **Warden Cochran's**
  abuse established in her own captions; the solitary flashback; Ksenia's *"Survive"*; the hidden
  boxcutter; the countdown to 18.
- **pp24–25** — [money page] **Victor's** office — the **Lozen** reveal (great-great-grandmother;
  Chiricahua; Victorio's War). *"You're — her."* The Willowbrook connection lands: *"I undid your
  restraints. You spoke."* *(pitch logic: pays off GN #1's Willowbrook opening — the saga rewards
  attention.)*
- **p26** — The guitar girl, **Laura** (15), introduced — Cochran hovering. *(plants GN #3/#4's rescue
  and the arrow.)*

**Nick & Carly — the long con of the heart**
- **p27** — Hell's Kitchen repair shop: Nick (20) vs. **Egan**; "Blacky Chan"; Egan's warning — *"the
  Feds are poking around… don't do anything STUPID."*
- **pp28–29** — Racetrack: *"you feeling lucky?"*; Carly picks **Daddy's Girl**; the 20-1 win; the
  "lucky charm" theory; Rio / "get the fuck out of this city — just for US."
- **pp30–31** — Arkadia basement gambling; Nick winning huge; the **police raid**; Arman hustles them
  out; Nick boosts a convertible — *"Top of the world, baby."* *(seeds GN #3's arrest/undercover turn.)*

**Junior — the chosen (the mythology's human face)**
- **p32** — 42nd St: Junior (15) + Wolfie/Cutty cut school to a **Bruce Lee triple feature**; the
  "enhancer." *(rights: Bruce Lee as a character — flagged.)*
- **pp33–34** — [money page] Junior trips into **XIA** — the ancient city; **Bruce Lee**: *"Pay
  attention."* The King & **Queen Goddess of Light** present the **golden child** — *"For we have been
  CHOSEN!"* Back in the theater: *"look at this dude TRIPPING his ASS off."* *(art note: rhyme the
  golden child with the apocalypse's five glowing figures.)*

**Harlem falls / Flick rises / T-Byrd**
- **pp35–36** — Harlem decaying; shuttered landmarks; **Nicky Barnes on Rikers**; **Flick's** crew
  taxing and torching mom-and-pop shops — *"Pay up or burn the fuck down."* Jade: *"NOBODY driving me
  outta MY home."*
- **p37** — **T-Byrd** at a burned body (Deakins) across from Buster's; the barbershop code of silence;
  Flick smug in the chair. *(the cop-vs-Flick thread that resolves in GN #3.)*

**The title drop**
- **p38** — [money page] Chuy at **Marvel**: *"we are VERY impressed. And LOVE the title. A Thunder of
  Dragons."* *(rights: the Marvel office scene may need a fictional publisher — flagged.)*

**Sean escalates / Lian**
- **p39** — The curio shop trashed; **Mr Feng** beaten; Sean's rage.
- **pp40–41** — The restaurant: the **rat prank** / fortune cookies / the **NY Times food critic**;
  Sean delighted; **Mr Zhao** catches Sean & Lian — *"You no kiss that boy!"* *(seeds GN #4's Lian
  split.)*

**Jay & Mrs Jones / Partners**
- **p42** — Jay & **Mrs Jones** ("we got a thing goin' on"); the airshaft red room; Chuy explains the
  **beeper**.
- **p43** — Chuy's **contract** — *"Five thousand… Partners… we open our own dojo — a SHO'JO… You the
  ONLY person ever see CHUY."*
- **p44** — [money page] Chuy sketches the **very first image of Jay as the Shogun of Harlem.** *(the
  birth of the icon — the cover-worthy page of this book.)*

**Genee**
- **p45** — Genee (16) growing up fast; Jay resolves to *"keep a real close eye on her."* *(seeds GN #3's
  bad night.)*

**The recruiters arrive (mythology closing in)**
- **p46** — JFK: **Master Lu**, Balo, Hoon, Shih land in New York.
- **p47** — 8th Ave: the peep-show barker; the monks bewildered — *"Only in AMERICA."*
- **pp48–49** — Empire State observation deck: *"To understand a world so different from our own, must
  we not seek the answer from those who inhabit it?"* Balo's warning of imbalance; *"let us look
  forward to tomorrow."*

*(Note on pacing: pp50–58 are intentionally light — reserve for Louis to expand any thread above he
wants to breathe. Book Two has the most give here; flag which threads he wants richer.)*

## ACT III — THE STORM UNHEARD (pp 59–78)

- **pp59–68** — Louis-expansion window: additional 1978 texture (Sean/Lian, Remi's countdown, Nick &
  Carly, Junior, the decaying Harlem) as Louis wants — the runway before the tournament. *(This block
  is deliberately flexible; see open question #2.)*
- **pp69–70** — The threads tighten toward a single night: everyone unknowingly a day from the
  tournament that will gather them.
- **p71** — [money page] Chuy sketches **five figures, arms to the sky, red lightning charging them** —
  a dead echo of the cold-open apocalypse (p7). Jay: fantasy, or premonition?
- **pp72–77** — Final movement: the storm montage (wolves, the monastery's red lightning, the Times
  Square sun blacking out) intercut with the kids' ordinary night; Master Lu contemplating the thing
  *"never done before."* Mrs Jones opens her door — *"grasshopper."*
- **p78** — [money page] Button/hook: *"we could not hear the thunder of dragons in the distance and did
  what we did…"* Final image points at tomorrow — the gathering. *(hook straight into GN #3's
  tournament.)*
