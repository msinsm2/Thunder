# A Thunder of Dragons — GN #4 ("The Choosing") — Page-by-Page Beat Sheet

**Adapts:** Volume One, **Book Four**
**Role:** The climax and the launch. Every lead reaches the line they swore not to cross; Master Lu's
scrolls summon the finalists; the crime web erupts; Buster quietly ends Flick; and the chosen fly to
China. The book that closes Volume One and opens the door to the whole Dragonverse.
**Status:** Working draft for Louis's review (canon authority). Beats and page counts are a proposal.

---

## Format assumptions

- **Target length:** ~**48 story pages** (the leanest, most propulsive book — center of a 44–56 range;
  can breathe toward the others' length if Louis wants more air on the goodbyes).
- **Splittable:** if serialized, breaks cleanly into **two ~24-page issues** — recommended break noted
  at the recruitment turn.
- **Two caption voices continue:** adult-Jay's narration + Remi's distinct captions.
- **How to read:** `p#` (ranges for sequences), with **[money page]**, *(art note)*, *(pitch logic)*.
- **Picks up directly** from GN #3's cliffhanger (Genee slammed by a car).

---

## What GN #4 must accomplish

1. **Pay off every thread** — Flick, Cochran, the Egan crime web, Sean & Lian, the Lozen bow — all land
   in one book.
2. **Force the choices** — each lead crosses (or refuses) their line; who they become is decided here.
3. **Answer the '83 prologue's promise** — the darkness Jay warned about begins to take root.
4. **Launch the saga** — the recruitment gathers the chosen and puts them on the jet to China.
5. **Close with a door, not a wall** — the ending must make Volume Two feel inevitable.

## ACT I — THE RAGE & THE RECRUITMENT (pp 1–14)

**The rage (a fake-out) (pp 1–6)**
- **pp1–4** — [money page] Jay's **blood-red revenge fantasy** — killing Flick in the pool hall, taking
  Flick's bullet — intercut with him furiously packing (*"Don't think — just PACK. I'd given Mama my
  word."*). *(art note: this is the '83 prologue's violence, rehearsed in his mind — plant the visual
  rhyme.)*
- **pp5–6** — The reveal: it's a **dream** — Mrs Jones's closet, **Mr Jones** home. Comic release after
  the nightmare, and a tonal exhale before the real crossings.

**The recruitment begins / the church (pp 7–14)**
- **pp7–8** — Spofford is locked down under Cochran; the hospital — Genee is stable. Nick's deal: he
  offers up **Tinsley**; **Carly-the-cop confirmed** behind the glass.
- **pp9–11** — The church: *"DARK TIMES up here in Harlem… WHAT CAN I DO TO STAND AGAINST THE DARK?"* On
  **Junior**, the question takes root. *(the thematic engine of the whole volume, stated aloud.)*
- **pp12–14** — The **scrolls** arrive — Hoon recruits **Chuy**, then **Sean**, then **Junior**; **Shih
  seeks Remi.** Jade to Chuy: *"get Jay on that plane… do not breathe a word of it to HIM."* *(If
  serializing, recommended two-issue break here.)*

## ACT II — CROSSING THE LINES (pp 15–34)

**Sean & Lian (pp 15–18)**
- **pp15–16** — Sean menaces **Mr Zhao** (the massage parlor) for Lian's whereabouts — *"There is
  DARKNESS inside you, Feng!"*
- **pp17–18** — [money page] **Port Authority**: Lian leaves by bus — *"there is another you in there…
  full of a rage that terrifies me."* Sean: *"this was YOUR choice."* *(Sean's turn toward the dark.)*

**Jay's choice (pp 19–24)**
- **pp19–21** — Hospital: the truth — Genee was **assaulted and drugged (Flick).** Jay overhears. Jade's
  plea: *"I am NOT gonna lose YOU like I did your Daddy… SWEAR to me, son."*
- **pp22–24** — Jay bolts for Flick; **Chuy tackles him** — *"This is not who we ARE, Jay!"*

**Remi's reckoning (pp 25–27)**
- **pp25–27** — [money page] Remi takes **Lozen's bow** and **kills Cochran with an arrow between the
  eyes**; she frees Laura — *"you keep singing, Laura Charles."* *(the payoff of the boxcutter, the
  guitar girl, and the Lozen lineage — all at once.)*

**The crossings converge (pp 28–34)**
- **p28** — Sean's **one-man war** vs. the Italians in the Mulberry St. crosswalk — bodies strewn; he
  walks away in a rage.
- **pp29–32** — [money page] **Café Figaro shootout**: Tinsley kills **Bearden**; **Nick kills
  Tinsley**; **Carly: "your father KILLED mine."** Nick walks out; Carly can't pull the trigger.
- **pp33–34** — The reveal that **Buster** gave T-Byrd the name — **Flick is arrested.** Jay & Chuy round
  the corner to see it; Jay's rage is defused by another man's quiet stand: *"Darkness wins when a nigga
  like me know better don't do what needs to be done."*

## ACT III — THE CHOOSING (pp 35–48)

- **pp35–37** — **Junior → Sifu Yu**; he gives his real name: *"I am — Leroy."* *(the chosen one set on
  his own path — a thread to pull in Volume Two.)*
- **pp38–40** — Loose threads settle: Carly and Egan's unfinished war (Egan nods at her — *not over*);
  the bloodied shoes stepping from a taxi; the graffiti "REMI…?" rolling past on an elevated train.
- **pp41–45** — [money page] The chosen — **Jay, Chuy, Remi, Sean, Nick** — converge at JFK from
  different directions like *"Reservoir Dragons."* Master Lu: *"in one way or another each of us HAD
  been chosen."*
- **pp46–48** — [money page] The **red jet** rumbles down the runway; city lights below. *"We were going
  to the Shaolin Temple… And we were about to find the fuck out."* **END OF VOLUME ONE.** *(the door
  into Volume Two.)*
