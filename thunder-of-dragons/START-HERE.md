## What this folder is

A working kit for turning **Volume One** into a sellable series of graphic novels: the original
manuscript, a story map, an adaptation plan, and page-by-page comic breakdowns — all in one place.

## Read in this order

1. **[`book-by-book-breakdown.md`](book-by-book-breakdown.md)** — *Start with the story.* A summary plus
   the major beats, in order, for all four internal Books (One → Four). The fastest way to hold the
   whole volume in your head.
2. **[`volume-one-breakdown.md`](volume-one-breakdown.md)** — *The plan.* How Volume One is cut into
   **three graphic novels**, with Book One as the standalone pitch object — plus the pitch strategy, a
   rights/clearance flag list, and a cast reference.
3. **The page-by-page beat sheets** (in [`beat-sheets/`](beat-sheets/)) — *The adaptation.* Each graphic
   novel broken into actual comic pages, with "money page" flags, art notes, and marked break points:
   - [`gn-1-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-1-beats.md) — GN #1, "Origins" (Book One) — the pitch object
   - [`gn-2-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-2-beats.md) — GN #2, "Coming of Age" (Book Two)
   - [`gn-3-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-3-beats.md) — GN #3, "The Tournament & The Choosing" (Books III+IV)
4. **[`manuscript/`](manuscript/)** — *The source.* Louis's original 178-page illustrated PDF (the
   source of record), plus its extracted script text and a short synopsis.

## The one big decision to weigh

**Three graphic novels, or four?** GN #3 currently fuses two Books, making it ~106 pp — about double #1
and #2. The cleanest alternative is to split Book Three and Book Four into their own volumes (four even
~50-pp books; the "Genee hit by a car" cliffhanger is the strongest seam in the volume). Both cuts are
laid out — with the exact break points marked — at the bottom of
[`gn-3-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-3-beats.md). *This is Louis's call.*

Each beat sheet also ends with **4 open questions** for Louis (page targets, the re-pointed GN #1
ending, the 3-vs-4-book split, and rights swaps).
