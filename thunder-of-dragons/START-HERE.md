# A Thunder of Dragons — Start Here

*Franchise workspace for **Volume One**, created by **Louis Venosta.** This one page routes you through
everything in the folder, in reading order.*

---

## What this folder is

A working kit for turning **Volume One** into a series of graphic novels: the original manuscript, a
story map, an adaptation plan, and page-by-page comic breakdowns — all in one place.

## The plan, in one line

**Volume One → four graphic novels, one per internal Book**, with **Book One as the standalone pitch
object** that sells the whole franchise.

| | Adapts | Working title | Approx. |
|---|---|---|---|
| **GN #1** | Book One | *Origins* (the pitch object) | ~72 pp |
| **GN #2** | Book Two | *Coming of Age* | ~78 pp |
| **GN #3** | Book Three | *The Tournament* | ~64 pp |
| **GN #4** | Book Four | *The Choosing* | ~48 pp |

## Read in this order

1. **[`book-by-book-breakdown.md`](book-by-book-breakdown.md)** — *Start with the story.* A summary plus
   the major beats, in order, for all four Books. The fastest way to hold the whole volume in your head.
2. **[`volume-one-breakdown.md`](volume-one-breakdown.md)** — *The plan.* The four-book cut, the GN #1
   pitch strategy, a rights/clearance flag list, and a cast reference.
3. **The page-by-page beat sheets** (in [`beat-sheets/`](beat-sheets/)) — *The adaptation.* Each graphic
   novel broken into actual comic pages, with "money page" flags, art notes, and break points:
   - [`gn-1-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-1-beats.md) — GN #1, *Origins* (Book One) — the pitch object
   - [`gn-2-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-2-beats.md) — GN #2, *Coming of Age* (Book Two)
   - [`gn-3-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-3-beats.md) — GN #3, *The Tournament* (Book Three)
   - [`gn-4-beats.md`](beat-sheets/gn-4-beats.md) — GN #4, *The Choosing* (Book Four)
4. **[`manuscript/`](manuscript/)** — *The source.* Louis's original 178-page illustrated PDF (the
   source of record), plus its extracted script text and a short synopsis.

## For Louis to weigh in on

Each beat sheet ends with a short list of **open questions** — page targets, the re-pointed GN #1
ending, and the rights swaps (the *Kung Fu*/Caine lineage, Bruce Lee, the real-person cameos, and the
Sho'Nuff / Shogun of Harlem tie to the 1985 film). Louis is the canon authority; everything here is a
proposal to react to.
